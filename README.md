# 💧 Water Quality Evaluation & Risk Identification Study (2025)

## 📌 Overview
This project focuses on analyzing river water quality across Maharashtra using environmental monitoring data.  
The dashboard evaluates drinking and irrigation suitability, identifies pollution hotspots, and highlights chemical and biological risks using Power BI.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Assess drinking water quality using WQI  
- Evaluate irrigation water suitability  
- Identify pollution hotspots and high-risk districts  
- Analyze chemical, biological, and salinity risks  
- Support sustainable water resource management  

---

## 📊 Tools & Technologies
- **Microsoft Excel**
  - Data Cleaning & Transformation  
  - Handling Missing Values  
  - Removing Duplicates  
  - Pivot Tables  

- **Power BI**
  - Data Modeling (Star Schema)  
  - DAX Calculations  
  - KPI Metrics  
  - Interactive Dashboards

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 📌 Descriptive
- 72% of drinking water samples are unsafe  
- Mumbai identified as the worst affected district  
- Significant presence of salinity and chemical stress  

### 📌 Diagnostic
- High BOD and low DO indicate organic pollution  
- High coliform levels suggest sewage contamination  
- Elevated TDS and sodium impact irrigation  

### 📌 Predictive
- Pollution trends may worsen without intervention  
- Increasing salinity may reduce crop productivity  

### 📌 Prescriptive
- Strengthen industrial effluent monitoring  
- Improve sewage treatment systems  
- Promote sustainable agriculture practices  
- Implement continuous water quality monitoring
